# Content Guidelines

## Tone
- Direct, clear, no fluff
- First person when Ron is speaking, third person when describing
- Confident but not boastful
- Technical audience assumed but not required

## Writing Rules
- Short sentences. Short paragraphs.
- Bold key phrases for scanners
- One idea per paragraph
- No jargon without context
- Links on first mention only, not every occurrence

## Hebrew
- ron.build content is in Hebrew
- WhatsApp group card uses Hebrew
- RTL handled by browser if `dir="rtl"` and `lang="he"` set
- Keep Hebrew content separate (dedicated sections/cards), don't mix mid-paragraph

## Image Sourcing
- AI-generated images: always note in alt text or caption
- Screenshots: blur names/photos of non-public people
- Hero image: `img/hero.webp` (AI-generated, 1536x1024)
- Compress all images before committing (target: <200KB)
- Prefer WebP format

## Page Structure
- Every page has: back link, h1, content, footer
- Footer includes: home link, Ron link, version
- Consistent nav across all pages

## Descriptions
- Tile/card descriptions: 5-8 words max
- Tooltip descriptions: 1 sentence (15-20 words)
- Full descriptions: 2-3 sentences on dedicated pages
- Meta descriptions: under 160 chars

## Don'ts
- No "click here" links
- No walls of text without structure
- No orphan pages (everything linked from somewhere)
- No placeholder text in production
