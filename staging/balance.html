Balance

A bipolar copilot app. Track mood, sleep, and energy patterns with AI-powered insights.

What is Balance?

Balance is a personal copilot for people living with Bipolar disorder. It combines mood tracking, sleep patterns, and energy levels with AI that learns your cycles and helps you stay ahead of episodes.
